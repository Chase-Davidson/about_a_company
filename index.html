The Art Peddler

Started the business in 2016 but have been painting her entire life. Art has always been a strong passion, and now I have hopes of turning it into something relative for you. I can work on any platform, from drawing a still portrait, to re-designing your antique furniture. All of which has been hand crafted by us. We are now able to offer furniture repair as well, if needed when refurbishing a piece for you.

This is my Mother's passion, which I have seen grow over the years from just your traditional painting, to now refurbishing furniture and creating a business around it. I am happy to help when I can as I will always want to see this business succeed.
